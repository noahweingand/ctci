Notes on resumes
    Length
        - Should be at 1 page max if you have < 10 years of experience.
        - Reviewers spend about 10 seconds on your resume, shorter the better.
        - Some reviewers refuse to read longer resumes.
    History
        - Try to make bullet points specific.
        - Generally, follow: what you did, how you did it, what the results were, and make it
          measurable somehow if you can.
    Projects
        - Include 2 - 4
        - Explain the project and what technologies/languages were used.
        - Reviewers prefer individual, out-of-school projects since it shows initiative.
    Languages/Software
        - List langauges you use but provide experience levels in parenthesis 
            - EX: Java (expert), Python (intermediate), C (beginner)
